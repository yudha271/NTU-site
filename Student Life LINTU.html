Life Begins In NTU

Step into our campus and you will find a young vibrant university beaming with life. In NTU, we believe learning continues beyond the four walls of the classroom, lecture theatre, and laboratory. Here you will find many opportunities to engage in activities that will help you develop holistically as an individual. Whether you choose to volunteer for a cause, pick up a new hobby or take on a leadership role, we have something for you.
In NTU, we believe in developing healthy, resilient men and women who are able to rise up to lifs challenges. We have an array of support services, from counselling and peer helping to academic mentoring and tutoring, to give you that extra bit of help whenever you need it.
